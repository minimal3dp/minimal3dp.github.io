{{< faq-item question="Are the calculators really free?" >}}
Yes! All our calculators are 100% free to use. The [FDM Cost Calculator](/tools/m3dp-fdm-cost-calculator/) and [Shrinkage Calculator](/tools/m3dp-shinkage-calculator/) run directly in your browser with no registration required.
