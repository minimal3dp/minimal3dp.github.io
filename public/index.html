Frequently Asked Questions

Q:What is Minimal 3DP?

A:Minimal 3DP is a comprehensive 3D printing resource created by Mike Wilson, featuring free calculators, Klipper tutorials, OrcaSlicer guides, printer reviews, and troubleshooting help. With over 5,000 YouTube subscribers, we provide tested solutions for makers of all skill levels.

Q:Are the calculators really free?

A:Yes! All our calculators are 100% free to use. The FDM Cost Calculator and Shrinkage Calculator run directly in your browser with no registration required.

Q:Do I need Klipper firmware to use these guides?

A:Not at all! While we have extensive Klipper calibration guides, most of our tutorials work with any 3D printer firmware including Marlin. Check out our OrcaSlicer tutorials for slicer-specific content.

Q:How can I get help with my 3D printing problem?

A:We offer FREE 15-minute consultations via Google Meet. Schedule here or contact minimal3dp@gmail.com for extended help sessions. You can also ask questions on our YouTube channel.

Q:What printers do you recommend for beginners?

A:Check our 3D printer reviews section for detailed breakdowns. We test printers hands-on and provide honest pros/cons for different use cases and budgets.
